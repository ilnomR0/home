Bidder

Bidder is a bidding application made for business cordinators for bidding applications. This app interfaces with google sheets, and goes through it mapping the bidding number with a name. Instead of calling out "number 423? do we have a number 423?", you just say "jen? Do we have a jen baker?".

In development applications

MQT Forms

This application is a presentation application that has an emphisis on your audience. This will include your average editing tools that you know and love from things like canva, google slides, and powerpoint, plus a ton more super cool features all on the web for your convienience. One major feature is the ability for live polls on the presentation itself. Either scan a QR code or go to the website link posted on your presentation, and have your audience fill out an anonomous form with the results being displayed live for all to see

This is for everybody. for meetings, classrooms, a large get together, whoever might need a presentation app with form intigration.


Paper

Paper is a staticially typed, OOP oriented programming language with explicit control over memory, native compilation, and a rich standard library (POSH) for application-level development.

this is for developers who want a language with the ultimate flexibility between low-level and high-level. For beginners and professionals alike, for people who want the quickest development and the quickest execution.
